## Security Takeaways
- Even simple misconfigurations can lead to security breaches  
- Awareness of threats is the first step in defending systems  
- Ethical hacking must always follow legal and educational guidelines

---

## Detection & Defense Thoughts (Purple Team)
- Monitoring for phishing attempts or malware activity is critical  
- User training and awareness programs help reduce human error  
- Implementing basic defenses (firewalls, antivirus, logging) mitigates simple attacks
